Croissant Crazy

Croissants originated in Vienna, Austria 🤨, but became famous in France. They are buttery, flaky, and come in various flavors 😋, making them a beloved pastry worldwide. Croissants are a symbol of French baking expertise and craftsmanship 👩‍🍳. Every January 30th, people celebrate Croissant Day, indulging in these delightful pastries.
